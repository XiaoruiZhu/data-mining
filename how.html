How to use this website

Learn concepts with “Why” in mind

You should read the content provided in each section and chapter; however, you should NOT TAKE THEM FOR GRANTED. We are living in an information era where our understanding of the world is constantly evolving. My content can easily become outdated. Therefore, it’s essential to consider why we should learn these techniques, the purpose behind the development of a particular technique, and the motivations of the pioneers who first developed or invented these methods.

We believe that critical thinking will inspire you to better understand these concepts.
