How to use EducateUs GPT

Learn concepts with “Why” in mind

You should read the content provided in each section and chapter; however, you should NOT TAKE THEM FOR GRANTED. We are living in an information era where our understanding of the world is constantly evolving. My content can become outdated one day. Therefore, it’s essential to learn why we should learn these techniques, the purpose behind the development of a particular technique, and the motivations of the pioneers who first developed or invented these methods, instead of remembering these concepts and tools.

We believe that critical thinking will inspire you to better understand these concepts. More importantly, the critical thinking will cultivate you to be able to develop or learn whatever new technologies in the future.

We believe that:

your questions will truly enhance your skills as a thinker, leader, and communicator.

How to use the EducateUs GPT interactive learning

Use preset questions first

We have preset some important questions that are closely related to the concepts. We recommend you try to submit these questions first. Then, you can communicate with the assistant from EducateUsGPT to obtain more information on the other aspects of the concepts.
